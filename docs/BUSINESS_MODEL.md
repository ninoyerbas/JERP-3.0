# JERP 3.0 Business Model

## Executive Summary

JERP 3.0 is a **SaaS construction ERP system** designed to be affordable and accessible for contractors of all sizes. Built by a contractor who understands the industry, it offers enterprise-level features at prices small contractors can afford.

---

## Target Market

### Primary: Small to Mid-Size Construction Contractors
- **Size:** 3-50 employees
- **Revenue:** $500K - $10M annually
- **Current Pain:** Expensive ERP systems ($20K-$50K/year) or manual processes (Excel, QuickBooks)
- **Need:** Professional tools for job costing, certified payroll, and prevailing wage compliance

### Secondary: Large Contractors & GCs
- **Size:** 50-150+ employees
- **Revenue:** $10M+ annually
- **Current Pain:** Legacy systems that don't integrate well, high per-user costs
- **Need:** Multi-company management, consolidation, white-label options

---

## Revenue Model

### Subscription-Based (SaaS)
All plans are **monthly or annual subscriptions** based on **employee count**, not user seats.

**Key Differentiator:** Employee-based pricing vs. traditional per-user pricing
- **Traditional ERP:** $100-150 per user/month Ã— 20 users = $2,000-3,000/month
- **JERP 3.0:** $299/month for up to 50 employees = Flat rate

- **AWS/Azure Hosting:** $500-800/month = $6,000-9,600/year

## Key Metrics (KPIs)

### Customer Metrics
- **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue):** Primary revenue indicator
- **ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue):** Year-over-year growth
- **Customer Count:** Total active subscriptions
- **ARPU (Average Revenue Per User):** Revenue per customer
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** Total revenue per customer
- **Churn Rate:** Monthly cancellation rate (target: <5%)

### Acquisition Metrics
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** Cost to acquire one customer
- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** Should be 3:1 or better
- **Trial Conversion Rate:** % of trials that convert to paid
- **Payback Period:** Time to recover CAC (target: <12 months)

### Operational Metrics
- **Gross Margin:** Revenue minus COGS (target: 75%+)
- **Operating Margin:** Revenue minus all costs (target: 50%+)
- **Infrastructure Cost per Customer:** Cloud costs / customers
- **Support Tickets per Customer:** Quality indicator

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Downtime:** Multi-region deployment, 99.9% uptime SLA
- **Data Loss:** Daily backups, disaster recovery plan
- **Security Breach:** Encryption, penetration testing, insurance

### Business Risks
- **Low Adoption:** Free trial, freemium model, money-back guarantee
- **High Churn:** Focus on customer success, proactive support
- **Competition:** Continuous innovation, customer feedback loop

### Financial Risks
- **Cash Flow:** Annual billing encouraged, maintain 6 months runway
- **Scaling Costs:** Variable infrastructure scales with revenue
